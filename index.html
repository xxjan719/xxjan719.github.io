Bios

I am currently a second-year student in the Mathematical Program at the University of Florida, working with Prof. Chunmei Wang in Gainesville, Florida. I earned my M.S. in Mathematics from Lanzhou University in 2023 under the supervision of Dr. Minghua Chen.

Research Interests:

My research lies in the field of Numerical Methods and Scientific Computing, with a particular focus on integrating mathematical modeling and computational techniques to advance scientific applications. I work on Scientific Machine Learning, Statistical Learning, and numerical analysis methods. I also develop computational models for applications in protein-protein binding and other biological systems, with interests in finite element methods and their applications.
